## Long English

X/Twitter reply threads are full of repetitive bot spam: bait text, random handles, contact funnels, adult-service spam, and copy-paste templates.

X Reply Janitor is an open-source browser extension that hides those likely bot replies locally in your browser. It does not use the X API, does not need an account token, and does not upload tweet content. You can tune the strength, whitelist accounts, and add custom keyword or regex rules.

GitHub: https://github.com/zoahdev/x-reply-janitor
Download: https://github.com/zoahdev/x-reply-janitor/releases/latest
